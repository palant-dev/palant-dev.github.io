MF Infostream

Web App

Experience at: Cutowl Srl
Customer: Gruppo Class
Role: Project Manager & Design Lead
Date: 2024

A responsive web application designed for MF Newswires S.r.l. to collect, manage, and distribute multimedia content and real-time agency feeds. The platform includes a configurable dashboard, advanced search, alerts, and user settings, currently live at mfinfostream.it.

My role

I was responsible for the design direction of the platform, ensuring usability and consistency across the responsive interface, while closely collaborating with backend, frontend, and project management teams.

Context and goals

MF Newswires needed a modern digital environment to unify multiple financial news feeds (MF Newswires, MT Newswires, Nasdaq) and make them easily accessible to professional users. The goal was to provide a flexible dashboard, powerful search, and timely alerts, all while maintaining reliability for a data-intensive audience that depends on speed and accuracy.

Responsibilities

  • Defined the design system and translated functional requirements into wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes.
  • Collaborated with backend and frontend developers to ensure seamless integration of design and functionality.
  • Coordinated with client stakeholders to validate requirements and adjust the interface for specific workflows.
  • Oversaw the responsive adaptation to ensure consistent user experience across desktop and mobile.
  • Contributed to the documentation and shared design deliverables via Figma and Zeplin.

Key features delivered

  • Configurable dashboard: up to 8 widgets displaying real-time news feeds.
  • Sidebar navigation with quick access to news sections and search.
  • Advanced search with improved keyword matching and result highlighting.
  • News detail views including side preview and full expanded article.
  • Alerts system for desktop push notifications based on custom filters.
  • Account and settings area allowing users to personalize alerts.
  • Responsive layout optimized for both desktop and mobile.
