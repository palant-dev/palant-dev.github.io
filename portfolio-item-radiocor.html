Radiocor

Web, iOS & Android App

Experience at: Cutowl Srl
Customer: Gruppo 24 ORE
Role: Project Manager & Design Lead
Date: 2024-2025

This project focused on redesigning and expanding a financial news application available on both web and mobile. The platform needed to be faster, easier to navigate, and equipped with new features while maintaining stability and security across devices. My role was to guide the process from planning to release, ensuring technical delivery aligned with business goals and user needs.

Alongside managing the overall project, I acted as a product manager by leading the design team. I coordinated UX decisions, defined priorities, and worked closely with designers to build an interface that felt consistent and intuitive whether accessed from desktop or smartphone.

I collaborated with developers, designers, and stakeholders to turn broad requirements into concrete tasks. Together, we introduced features such as customizable alerts, advanced search and filtering tools, and responsive layouts optimized for every screen. I also oversaw the integration of video content and structured sections for news, dividends, and corporate actions, making sure information was easy to find and well organized.

Beyond timelines and priorities, I focused on communication. Acting as the link between client and technical teams, I ensured decisions were understood and documented, reducing friction during development. During testing, I supervised checks on authentication, subscription access, and performance to guarantee a smooth launch on both platforms.

The result was a modernized application, stable and reliable across web and mobile, with improved usability and personalization. It not only enhanced the day-to-day experience of financial professionals but also laid the groundwork for future evolutions of the product.
