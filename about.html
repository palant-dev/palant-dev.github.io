ABOUT ME

Hello, I’m Antonio Palomba, a Project Manager with a program mindset and a strong foundation in iOS and web development. I’m passionate about building software that is not only reliable and scalable but also meaningful for the people who use it.

My journey into technology began at a young age, experimenting with computers and design tools. Over time, my curiosity evolved into a professional path that blends technical expertise with program management. I hold a degree in Computer Science and have applied my skills in environments ranging from 3D modeling and digital media to consulting and large-scale software delivery.

Today, I lead multiple projects in parallel, ensuring they are delivered on time, on budget, and aligned with strategic goals. I specialize in:

  • Program & Project Management — planning releases, tracking risks and dependencies, and driving cross-functional alignment with Agile practices.
  • Software Development Life Cycle — hands-on experience in iOS (SwiftUI, UIKit, MVVM, Core Data) and modern web technologies.
  • Technical Communication — bridging engineering, design, and product to ensure clarity, shared context, and measurable outcomes.

I’ve worked with international teams, mentored peers, and contributed to transformative initiatives such as the Apple Developer Academy, combining app building with collaborative leadership.

When I’m not leading projects or coding, I enjoy reading, exploring new technologies, and writing about delivery frameworks and program leadership.

Welcome to my world, where program management, technology, and innovation converge.
